    INTRODUCTION TO URS..

    The prime aim of the Department of Electronics is to impart quality teaching & learning and provide research facilities in the field of electronics to meet the increasing demand of highly skilled technical personnel in the electronics industry.

    B.Sc (H) Electronics course is designed to enhance the student's understanding of scientific principles and to further develop their technical skills by hands-ondesigning and testing of electronic circuits and systems similar to the that ones are used in industry.

    In its endeavor to keep itself abreast with new knowledge and developments in the field, the department routinely organizes workshops and training programs for the students and faculty members.


    B.Sc. (H) Electronics Course: The course curriculum is a perfect blend of papers in physics, mathematics and computer with special emphasis on electronics. Students are also trained in fundamental computational skills to use techniques and software for simulation, data analysis and visualization more efficiently with emphasis on integrated approach to electronics and computer science, taught using substantial laboratory experiments in areas such as analog, digital, signal and systems, communication, opto-electronics, microprocessor and micro-controller.

    Electronics graduates and post graduates have a bright future ahead of them in various areas like Design, Development, Fabrication, Inspection, Quality control and Maintenance.


    Activities during the academic year 2017-18: The student’s society of the department “TechTitan” organized its annual festival “ElectroZeal - A Culmination of Talents!” comprising of both academic and cultural events on 26th of February 2018. The ElectroZeal – 2018, comprised of 08 formal and informal events to bring to fore the various dormant potential of students. The event attracted more than 200 teams in various events of the festival from different colleges of the University of Delhi. About 350 students from various colleges across the Delhi- NCR region showcased an amazing array of talents.


    Workshops and Seminars: The department organized a seminar cum workshop on the topic “Image Processing using OpenCV & Python” so that the students get the knowledge by listening to the experts and give exposure beyond their curriculum. A one day seminar on “Recent developments in electronics and Industrial applications” was held on 15th March 2018. The students interacted with academicians and industrialists and shared their real time experiences related to the current developments in the electronic industry. A workshop cum demonstration of “Precision Agricultural Techniques” was organized by the students of the department from 19th-21st January 2018, at Village Baroji, Haryana for the benefits of the farming community.

    In an attempt to make our students more sensitized towards environmental issues and conservation of wildlife, the department in collaboration with History and Economics Departments of the college organized a National Conference on “Biodiversity and Climate Change - Wildlife Ecology, Conservation and Iconography” from 8th – 10th February 2018. The department of Electronics hopes to continue with its endeavors in the coming years and provide holistic education and support to its students
